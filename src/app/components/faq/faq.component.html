Orders and Returns

Tracking my order

You may track your item by clicking the tracking number provided in your email.

I haven’t received my order

If your tracking number shows your package was delivered, but you did not receive it, please follow these steps: Check with any other members of your household to see if someone else might have accepted the delivery on your behalf.
1/ Check with neighbors to see if your package was accidentally delivered to the wrong address.
2/ Search all around the area you usually receive deliveries, or with a front desk/receptionist if applicable.
3/ If you still have not found your package, please contact us at roboshop@shoprevelry.com and notify us as soon as possible, and no later than 7 days after the stated delivery date.

How can I return an item?

You can cancel your order within 12 hours upon placing your order. To cancel your order, please email us for us to process them for you. Unfortunately, any orders past 12 hours will be registered onto our system and we will not be able to cancel them.
